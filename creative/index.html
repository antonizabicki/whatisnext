max for live devices
i Max for Live devices are custom instruments and effects that extend Ableton Live's capabilities, created by the community.

HAAZZ™

A simple Haas effect device with added dry/wet control, allowing you to create more stereo width and a controlled sense of chaos.

MEEDSIDE™

A straightforward mid/side splitter, letting you separate and process the center (mid) and sides of your stereo signal independently.

MEEDYSHEEFT™

A MIDI shifter with added octave modulation buttons, perfect for exploring different pitches and finding the right sound without editing the original MIDI.

A real-time oscilloscope for viewing waveforms, featuring adjustable sampling and gain. It displays mids in blue and sides in orange.
